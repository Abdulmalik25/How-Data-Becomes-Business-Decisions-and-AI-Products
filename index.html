Why this matters

These roles aren't separate job titles. They're one connected system.

Most explanations teach data careers as a list: "here's what a Data Analyst does, here's what a Data Scientist does." That's why so many students and career-switchers get stuck picking a role — they're comparing job titles with no sense of how the work actually connects.

In reality, every role sits on the same value chain. A Data Engineer creates reliable pipelines and data layers, Analysts and BI Developers turn trusted data into insight, Data Scientists and ML Engineers turn it into predictive systems, and AI Engineers turn company data and knowledge into AI products. Understanding where you'd sit in that flow — not just what the title sounds like — is what makes a career choice make sense.
