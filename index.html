Why this matters

These roles aren't separate job titles. They're one connected system.

Most explanations teach data careers as a list: "here's what a Data Analyst does, here's what a Data Scientist does." That's why so many students and career-switchers get stuck picking a role — they're comparing job titles with no sense of how the work actually connects.

In reality, every role sits on the same pipeline. A Data Engineer's pipeline feeds an Analytics Engineer's models, which feed an Analyst's report and a Scientist's prediction, which an AI Engineer turns into a product a customer actually touches. Understanding where you'd sit in that flow — not just what the title sounds like — is what makes a career choice make sense.
